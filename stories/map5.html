Ghostbuster's Guide to Dhaka City

Last year, I had planned to make a map of eriee locations across Bangladesh, but it never quite came together. This Halloween, though, @esrigram invited the geospatial community to explore haunted maps, sparking me to finally give it a try. After some effort, the concept came together nicely. So, here’s a bit of haunted geography:

"Historically, Bengal’s spirits seemed at ease dwelling in large trees and water. In the days of abandoned palaces, they roamed freely and dwelled in those, but as times changed and empty palaces turned into condomoniums and faded from memory, these spirits began to mingle with humans. Do ghosts and humans coexist in this way elsewhere in the world? Hard to say. But in Bengal, they have found their way into human life."
